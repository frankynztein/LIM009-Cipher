Choose your language:




It's pretty simple. Once you've selected your language, you'll see this screen.



As you can see, you have three areas. The first one is for you to code. Do you have any message you don't want the wrong person to read? Then, enter your message, select a key (a number) and click Encode.



Perhaps somebody gave you a message with very important information and a key for you to decypher. That middle area is the one for you.



In the last area you'll see your encoded/decoded message. Here you can copy it and share it. Easy peasy, right?
